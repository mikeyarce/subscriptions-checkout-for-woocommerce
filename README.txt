Allows your customers to sign-up at Checkout to receive your latest posts via email, using Jetpack Subscriptions.

== Description ==

This plugin adds a checkbox to your site's Checkout process that lets your customers opt-in to receive your site's latest news via email, using Jetpack's Subscription module.

For this plugin to work, you need to have [Jetpack](https://wordpress.org/plugins/jetpack/) installed, activated, and connected to WordPress.com.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Unzip the archive and put `subscriptions-checkout-for-woocommerce` into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= I don't see the checkbox added to my Checkout =

Make sure you are running the latest version of [Jetpack](https://wordpress.org/plugins/jetpack/) and [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) and that your Jetpack is connected to your WordPress.com account.

= My customers are not receiving my posts in their email =

The most likely case is that they are going into a Spam folder, or they unsubscribed.  Ask them to check their WordPress.com Subscriptions from their WordPress.com Dashboard, and to check their Spam/Junk mail folders.
